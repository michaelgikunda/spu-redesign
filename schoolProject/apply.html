Admissions Criteria
To be considered for admission to St. Paul's University, you must meet the following minimum requirements:

1. High school diploma or equivalent
2. Minimum GPA of 2.5
3. English proficiency (for non-native English speakers)
In addition to these requirements, some programs may have additional admissions criteria.
